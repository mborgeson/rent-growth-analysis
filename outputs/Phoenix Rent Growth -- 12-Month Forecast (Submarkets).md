## Data Sources & Methodology

### **Primary Data Sources**

- **FRED API:** National macroeconomic variables (mortgage rates, inflation, Fed policy)
- **BLS (Bureau of Labor Statistics):** Employment by sector and MSA/submarket
- **Census Bureau:** Building permits, population estimates, migration (MSA level)

- **GreatSchools:** School district ratings
- **Arizona State University:** Enrollment data
- **IRS:** County-to-county migration data (MSA level, estimated for submarkets)

### **Model Framework**

**Hierarchical Ensemble Components:**

1. **National Macro VAR Model (30% weight)**
   - Forecasts baseline apartment demand environment
   - Variables: 30-year mortgage rates, employment growth, household formation, inflation expectations
   - Captures interdependencies between national variables

2. **Phoenix-Specific Gradient Boosted Trees (45% weight)**
   - Captures local market dynamics and non-linear interactions
   - Variables: Employment by sector, supply pipeline, CA migration, SFH prices, ASU enrollment
   - Submarket-level coefficients for heterogeneity

3. **Seasonal SARIMA Baseline (25% weight)**
   - Ensures ensemble captures lease renewal seasonality
   - Phoenix exhibits modest spring/summer peaks (1-2pp variation)

**Dynamic Weight Updating:**
- Weights adjust based on recent forecast errors
- Allows ensemble to adapt when Phoenix diverges from national trends
- Example: 2020-2022 pandemic migration surge increased GBM weight to 60%

### **Variable Selection Process**

**Stage 1: Elastic Net Regularization**
- 68 candidate variables ‚Üí 18 selected variables
- Time series cross-validation (10 folds, 12-month forecast)
- L1 (lasso) + L2 (ridge) regularization

**Stage 2: Permutation Importance**
- Rank variables by true predictive contribution
- 30 shuffles per variable for statistical confidence
- Measures degradation when variable relationship broken

### **Validation Methodology**

**Time Series Cross-Validation:**
- Rolling 24-month training window
- 12-month forecast horizon
- 10 folds (no future data leakage)

**Performance Metrics:**
- **MAPE:** Mean Absolute Percentage Error
- **RMSE:** Root Mean Squared Error
- **R¬≤:** Coefficient of Determination

**Confidence Intervals:**
- Conformal prediction (distribution-free)
- 80% coverage guarantee
- Validated on calibration set
